A Legacy of Flavor

Founded in the heart of Sri Lanka's cinnamon country, CEYNOVA CINNAMON began as a small family farm dedicated to producing the world's finest Ceylon cinnamon. Over 15 years, we have grown into a trusted export partner while maintaining our commitment to traditional harvesting methods and sustainable practices.

Every stick of cinnamon we produce carries the warmth of Sri Lankan sunshine and the care of generations of farming expertise.

Our Mission

To deliver the purest Ceylon cinnamon and premium Sri Lankan spices to the world, while empowering local farming communities and preserving our rich agricultural heritage through sustainable and ethical practices.

We believe that exceptional quality starts at the source — from the soil to your table.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted source for authentic Ceylon cinnamon, recognized globally for uncompromising quality, sustainability, and the preservation of Sri Lanka's spice heritage for future generations.

We envision a world where every kitchen experiences the true essence of Ceylon cinnamon.
